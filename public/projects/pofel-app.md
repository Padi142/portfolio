## TLDR

Mobile app for planning and organizing events with friends. The app allows you to create events, invite friends, chat with them and see who is coming. Offers quality of life features like a shared inventory, where everyone can see what other people are bringing, quest system so you dont forget anything and a shared photo library . The app is built using [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/) and [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/). Over it's lifetime, the app has been downloaded over 10k times and has an active user base.
